Data Set Purpose:

This data set contains information about individuals who have an "employment event",
where an "employment event" means either starting a new job or retiring. In addition
to capturing some personal and demographic information, the data set contains details 
of the individual's educational background and work experience history. Only the most
recent "employment event" for an individual is captured.

Attribute Descriptions:
rec_id                  The record identifier
ssn:			The social security number of the individual (Unique).
first_name:		The first given name of the individual.
middle_name:		The second given name of the individual.
last_name:		The surname or family name of the individual.
gender:			The person's gender (m/f).
current_age             The current age of a person at the "employment event". 
birth_date:		The person's date of birth.
street_address:		The first part of the address containing street number/street name.
suburb:			The suburb of the address.
postcode:		The postcode of the address.
state:			The state of the address.
phone:			The individual's home phone number.
email:			The individual's personal e-mail address.
education:		The highest level of education obtained by the individual.
occupation:		The industry or job category at the "employment event".
salary:			The salary at the "employment event".
credit_card_number:	The credit card number of the individual.
years_of_experience:	The number of years the individual worked in the field (or their former field for retired individual's).
employment_timestamp:	The date of the "employment event".
